twitterspy by dustin

TwitterSpy is a supplemental XMPP bot for twitter that does the stuff the twitter one used to do, and a few things it didn't.

IM help to twitterspy@jabber.org to see what you can do.

When Messages Cease

Twitterspy will stop doing work for you, and stop sending you messages under the following conditions:

  • You are not logged in to your XMPP server.
  • You are logged in with a negative priority.
  • You are logged in with your XMPP status as xa (extended away) or dnd (do not disturb)
  • You have specifically disabled the service via the "off" command.
